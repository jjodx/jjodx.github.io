Le Neurone moteur


Neurone doesn’t need any translation from French… It shows my interest for neuroscience-related topics.

A moteur (aka an Engine)is what powers the movement of a vehicle. Here, it refer to the neurons that make the upper limb move.

Together, the “Neurone moteur” would be a very bad french translation of “motor neuron”but I chose it because I am interested how neurons of the brain drive movements, espcially of the upper limb.
